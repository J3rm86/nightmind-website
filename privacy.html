Introduction

NightMind ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

Privacy First: NightMind is designed with privacy at its core. Your dream journal data is stored locally on your device and encrypted. We do not collect, store, or have access to your personal data or dream content.

Information Storage

All data stays on your device:

  • Dream Entries: Stored locally in an encrypted database on your device
  • Voice Recordings: Processed entirely on your device and deleted after transcription
  • Settings and Preferences: Stored locally on your device

We do not have access to your dream content, and it is never uploaded to our servers for storage.

What We Do NOT Collect

  • We do NOT collect analytics or usage data
  • We do NOT track your behavior in the app
  • We do NOT store your dream content on our servers
  • We do NOT sell or share any personal information

Cloud Features (Optional)

When you choose to use AI-powered features (dream analysis or video generation), your dream text is temporarily sent to our secure servers for processing:

  • Data is transmitted securely using encryption
  • Processing occurs on Cloudflare Workers
  • Your dream text is NOT stored after processing is complete
  • No personal identifiers are attached to the processing requests

Microphone Permission

NightMind requests microphone access to enable voice recording of your dreams. This feature is entirely optional. When you use voice recording:

  • Audio is processed locally on your device using on-device AI
  • Voice recordings are NEVER uploaded to external servers
  • Audio files are automatically deleted after transcription

Third-Party Services

We use minimal third-party services:

  • RevenueCat: For processing subscriptions and purchases (handles payment data securely)
  • Cloudflare: For secure API hosting when using AI features

These services have their own privacy policies and do not receive your dream content.

Data Security

Your data is protected by:

  • Local encryption of your dream database
  • Secure HTTPS connections for any cloud features
  • No cloud storage of personal data

Your Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • Export your dream journal at any time
  • Delete all data from within the app
  • Use the app entirely offline (except for AI features)

Children's Privacy

NightMind is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date."
