Introduction

NightMind ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide:

  • Dream Entries: Text content of dreams you record in the app
  • Voice Recordings: Audio recordings when you use the voice-to-text feature (processed locally on your device)
  • Tags and Categories: Labels you assign to your dreams

Automatically Collected Information:

  • Device Information: Device type and operating system version for app compatibility
  • Usage Data: Anonymous analytics to improve app performance

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide dream journaling and analysis features
  • To generate AI-powered dream interpretations and videos
  • To process in-app purchases and subscriptions
  • To improve our services and user experience

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage: Your dream entries are stored locally on your device in an encrypted database. We do not have access to your dream content unless you explicitly use cloud features.

Voice Processing: Voice recordings are processed entirely on your device using on-device AI. Audio data is never transmitted to external servers.

Cloud Features: When you use AI analysis or video generation features, dream text is sent to our secure servers (Cloudflare Workers) for processing. This data is not stored permanently and is deleted after processing.

Microphone Permission

NightMind requests microphone access to enable voice recording of your dreams. This feature is optional. Voice recordings are:

  • Processed locally on your device
  • Converted to text using on-device AI (Whisper)
  • Never uploaded to external servers
  • Deleted after transcription is complete

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • RevenueCat: For subscription and purchase management
  • Cloudflare: For secure API hosting and content delivery

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share anonymized, aggregated data for analytical purposes.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data stored in the app
  • Export your dream journal
  • Delete all your data from the app
  • Opt out of analytics collection

Children's Privacy

NightMind is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date."
